Trip Designer Case Study

Trip Designer represents an in-class case study that I led from discovery research, through definition and ideation, into wireframe prototyping and iteration.

In-depth Interviews

The goal of this research is to better understand how people currently approach the process of trip planning in order to identify opportunities to improve the experience.

Empathy Map

Using learnings from our interviews, we created an empathy map to visualize attitudes and behaviors we now understood about our target user.

User Persona

Lastly, we created Hilary, a representative persona that the design team could have as someone to refer back to during the design process as decisions were being made.

Problem Statement

Organized, enthusiastic travelers often find themselves overburdened when managing and coordinating their itineraries during trips. This takes time away them having an authentic, enjoyable experience in a new place. How might we enable travelers to minimize time spent coordinating day-of and maximize time spent enjoying themselves?

Lo-fi wireframe prototype

After storyboards, charting a user flow and sketching out designs, I created a wireframe prototype in Figma.

Usability and Iterations

We completed a round of usability testing with the prototype and iterated on the designs based on our learnings.

Updated Prototype

Lastly, an updated prototype was made. This could be taken to another round of usability testing, or given a UI design polish to bring into a more finalized state.
